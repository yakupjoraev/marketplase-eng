Questions and answers.

Frequently asked questions.

I want to run a Tournament in a game that's not on your platform, when will you support it?

Please ask us when or if your preferred game might be available. In general, the more organizers and players ask for a game, the more leverage we have together when approaching game developers to provide integration alternatives.

Does it cost anything to use Challengermode?

It's free to create a Space and host Tournaments or other competitions as an organizer on Challengermode for your own community. If you wish to reach more users on our platform through targeted promotions or are looking for custom solutions, please get in touch with our sales and partnerships team here: partner@challengermode.com

Is it possible to integrate Challengermode Tournaments to an external website?

Yes, there are two ways to integrate with Challengermode. You can use our Developer API to fetch player, match, and Tournament data from your Space and create a custom integration. Or use the live data embeds (iFrames) to show Challengermode Leaderboard, League, and Tournament widgets like scoreboards on your website.

Are there any requirements for becoming an organizer on Challengermode?

Anyone can create a Tournament or Space for free on Challengermode. If you want help with organizing your Tournament or have questions about how to use Challengermode, you're welcome to contact our esports team at esports@challengermode.com or our on-site support chat.

Some games or features may be limited to partners or pre-approved users. Should you wish to access these, please get in touch with the esports team via the above email address.

Why is Challengermode the right platform to host my/our esports competitions?

We created Challengermode because we believe that esports needs to become more accessible to both regular gamers and organizers, for the industry to reach its full potential. The goal is to offer a platform that doesn't just provide the tools to organize Tournaments, but makes it so easy that you can focus on what you actually want to do — grow your audience, create amazing esports experiences for viewers and players, and maybe most importantly, make money doing so.

What Tournament formats does Challengermode support?

Challengermode supports the most common formats like single and double elimination, round robin groups, Swiss and combinations of these such as a group stage feeding into a single elimination playoff stage. In addition, the League system handles several divisions played in a round robin format with automatic promotion and relegation every season.

How do I create a Tournament on Challengermode?

Anyone can create an automated, so-called "User-Hosted Tournaments" using the Simplified Tournament System. If you wish to create custom Tournaments and access more advanced organizer features for monetization and community engagement, you must first create your own Space.

Some games or features may be restricted to pre-approved organizers. Please get in touch with us if you wish to access these additional esports tools and capabilities.

Frequently asked questions.

Does it cost anything to integrate with Challengermode?

It's currently free to integrate with the API but there are rate limits in place. Get started by exploring the docs. Certain features and capabilities may only be available to paying partners. Reach out to our partnerships team to learn more about our paid features.

What can I do with the Developer API?

You're currently able to extract information about Spaces, tournaments and individual matches from Challengermode. To integrate a game with the API directly, reach out to our partnerships team.

Can I get in-game stats from the Challengermode API?

Unfortunately not. Due to policies and terms of use of the game developer APIs, we're currently unable to provide detailed in-game stats (e.g. kills per player in Leage of Legends).
