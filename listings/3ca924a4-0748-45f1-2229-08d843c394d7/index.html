Tournament information

Metanoia Cup is an online tournament organized by Challengermode. In this season, the tournament will be played on all servers. Four teams from the Play-In will join the 5 directly invited teams in the Group Stage. Group Stage is a single round robin tournament. Top 4 teams will advance to the upper bracket while 5th-8th place teams will proceed to the lower bracket of the Playoffs. Bottom team will be eliminated. The Playoffs will be a double elimination bracket.
